Fundamental Vibrations: Different vibrational patterns of these strings correspond to various subatomic particles, such as electrons, quarks, and photons. A specific mode of vibration is identified as an electron.

Types of Strings: The theory includes open strings (with endpoints) and closed strings (loops).
